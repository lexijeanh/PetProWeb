DayCare:

Our daycare is offered Monday - Saturday from 6am to 7pm. It is provided so that your pet has a fun filled day full of socialization and activities. All pets are grouped according to their size, age, temperment and activity level in order to best suit their needs.

Training:

We offer training throughout your pets stay. We use games, treats, and toys as a motivational way for your pet to learn especially throughout the beginning stages. Eventually, your pet will learn without those tricks and find new things to keep them motivated.

Lodging:

While your pet stays with us, we care for them as if they are our own family. They will be loved and played with at all times. You will be able to watch yoru pet throughout their entire stay using our cameras set up throughout the entire lodge and outdor play areas. We also offer the abiltity to have your pet wear a goPro camera, therefore, you will be able to feel connected to your pet as though you are right there with them.
